🔥 Hot Take — Cold Start

5 min
No prep. No slides. Present your results — 60 seconds NOW.
Cold start: El alumno habla de resultados reales de su trabajo. Si se bloquea: "Did you achieve the target? — Yes or no? Then say it!" Push para Simple Past + results verbs.
Error a vigilar: "We exceed" → "We exceeded". Corrige al final.
🔥 Hot Take — 60 sec
You are Valeria Torres. Your manager just asked: "Can you give us a 60-second update on Q3 results?" GO — production, delivery, costs. No notes. Simple Past only.

§2

🎯 Pitch — Assigned Sides

10 min
Defend your side — even if you personally disagree
Topic: "Business presentations at Mexican companies should always be in Spanish, not English." Teacher asigna lados. Push para que usen Simple Past + results verbs en sus argumentos: "We achieved better results when we presented in Spanish…" / "The last presentation in English was very clear for the international team…"
Timer: 90 segundos por lado, luego 60 segundos de debate abierto.
🟣 Side A — SPANISH ALWAYS
"Presentations should always be in Spanish. Defend this for 90 seconds."
Starters: "When we presented in Spanish, the team understood everything clearly…" "We achieved better results when everyone understood the data…" "Didn't we miss important points in the last English presentation?"
🔵 Side B — ENGLISH FOR BUSINESS
"English presentations are essential. Counter the argument."
Starters: "The Chicago team didn't understand the Spanish report last quarter…" "We gained two international contracts after the English presentation…" "Wasn't the English version more professional for the investor meeting?"
§3

🔄 Relay — Transform the Sentence

8 min
No grammar table. No notes. Just transform and say it.
Dinámica: Teacher lee la oración → alumno transforma en voz alta. Énfasis en Simple Past, negative forms, y negative questions. Sin papeles.
Si se bloquea: "Is it positive or negative? OK — use 'didn't' + base verb."
Transform 1 — Positive → Negative
"We exceeded the delivery target."
→ Say the negative version. The result was bad.
"We didn't exceed the delivery target." (or "We missed the delivery target.")
Transform 2 — Statement → Negative Question
"The presentation was on Monday."
→ Make it a negative question. You sound surprised it wasn't Monday.
"Wasn't the presentation on Monday?" — Was/Were → Wasn't/Weren't in negative questions.
Transform 3 — Fix the error
"Didn't you exceeded the sales target last quarter?"
→ There's an error. Say the correct sentence.
"Didn't you exceed the target?" — Base verb after didn't. NEVER -ed after didn't.
Transform 4 — Add a reason
"We didn't reach the cost-reduction goal."
→ Add "because + reason" in Simple Past. Use a realistic workplace reason.
"We didn't reach the goal because raw material prices increased in August." (any past reason is valid)
Transform 5 — Make it yours
"Valeria improved efficiency by 12% in Q3."
→ Replace Valeria with yourself + a real result from your job.
Open — "I [exceeded / improved / reduced / gained]… by [%] last [quarter / month / year]."

🎤 Solo Challenge — 2 Minutes

8 min
Two minutes. Your results. No notes. Full presentation structure.
Solo: El alumno da una presentación de resultados REAL de su área. Teacher juega el rol de manager — hace una negative question durante la presentación ("Didn't you say the target was 90%?" / "Wasn't the delivery problem in August?"). Badge 🎤 si completa 2 minutos con estructura y Simple Past.
🎤 SOLO — Your Q3 Results Presentation (2 min)
Present your real results — or imagine you are Valeria Torres.
Open: "I'd like to present…" 2+ positive results (Simple Past) 1 negative result + reason Negative question from audience Close: "To summarize…"
§6

🔍 Source Analysis — Meeting Minutes & Slides

8 min
Real corporate documents — read, analyze, answer
Dinámica: Alumno lee documentos reales → analiza → responde. Tres tipos de fuente: minutes (formal), slide summary (visual), email chain (informal). Cada uno tiene una tarea analítica diferente.
📋 Meeting Minutes — Q3 Ops Review
Solaris Manufacturing · Celaya · Thursday 17 October · Attendees: Valeria Torres, Daniel Ochoa, Carlos Rivera
Results Discussed
▸Production exceeded target by 8%. Team improved efficiency after July training.
▸Delivery didn't reach 90% on-time target. Only 82% was achieved. Supplier delay was the main cause.
▸Costs reduced by 4%. Company gained efficiency in transport.
Action Points
▸Valeria will contact supplier by 25 Oct.
▸Carlos will prepare Q4 delivery plan.
❓ How many Simple Past verbs are in the "Results Discussed" section?
📊 Slide 3 of 5 — Q3 Summary
Valeria Torres · Solaris Manufacturing Q3 Presentation
Q3 RESULTS — OVERALL SUMMARY
✅ Production: exceeded target (+8%)
⚠️ Delivery: didn't reach 90% target (82% achieved)
✅ Costs: reduced by 4%
Key issue: supplier delay
❓ Valeria uses "didn't reach" for delivery. Why not "didn't reached"?
📧 Email Chain — Post-Presentation
From: Daniel Ochoa · To: Valeria Torres · Re: Q3 Presentation
"Hi Valeria, great presentation today. Quick question: Didn't you mention a 10% improvement in efficiency, not 8%? Also, wasn't the supplier contract renewed last month? I want to make sure the minutes are correct. — Daniel"
❓ Daniel uses two negative questions. Which form does he use for the verb "mention" — and why?
🧠 Grammar Detective — Find the Pattern
Look at these 4 sentences from the presentation and answer the question.
A) "The results were mostly positive."
B) "We exceeded the target by 8%."
C) "We didn't reach the delivery goal."
D) "Didn't you mention a 10% improvement?"
❓ Which sentence uses "was/were" — and which sentences use "did/didn't"? What's the rule?
