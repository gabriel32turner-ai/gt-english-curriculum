§ 01
Warm-Up — Negative Speed Round
5 min

Sin modelos. El maestro da el contexto. El alumno produce la oración negativa + expresión de duración INSTANTÁNEAMENTE. Haz clic en la tarjeta cuando hayas respondido. ⚡

S1
You worked on a report but couldn't finish it.
"I didn't ___ it. I worked on it ___."
✓
S2
Your team had a 3-hour meeting but made no decision.
"We didn't ___. The meeting lasted ___."
✓
S3
A client emailed you but you had meetings all day.
"I didn't ___. I was in meetings ___."
✓
S4
The supplier never sent the documents you needed.
"We didn't ___. We waited ___."
✓
S5
You tried to resolve a complaint but worked until late.
"I didn't ___. I worked on it ___."
✓
S6
BONUS — Use an IRREGULAR negative: didn't go / didn't have / didn't make.
"I didn't ___ [irregular] because I ___."
✓
Quick check: ¿El alumno dice "I didn't finished"? → Corrección inmediata: didn't + BASE siempre. ¿"During three hours"? → Solo es for.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • S6 es el diagnóstico clave: ¿el alumno produce espontáneamente un negativo con irregular (didn't go, didn't have, didn't make)? Si sí → consolidación S3+S4 exitosa. Si no → 3 min de drill adicional antes de role play.
  • Exige datos REALES. S2: "Your team" → el equipo real del alumno. ¿Hubo una reunión así esta semana?
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "6 situaciones, 6 oraciones reales de tu trabajo. Sin notas. Eso es exactamente lo que le dirías a tu director mañana en inglés."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "S5 pone todo junto: regular verbs + irregulars + negatives + Wh- questions + duration. Hoy es el último drilling antes del Exit Task."

⚡ Duration Expression Drill oral — 6 rounds
El maestro dice una duración: "for 3 hours" → el alumno hace una oración negativa REAL de su trabajo usando esa expresión. Sin repetir la misma actividad dos veces. Mínimo 6 rondas con las 6 expresiones: for / from…to / all morning / all afternoon / until / most of the day.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Flashcard 6 (irregular) es la más importante: "I didn't go to the meeting." Si el alumno dice "I didn't went" → corrección inmediata, el alumno repite correctamente antes de continuar.
  • Duration Drill: si el alumno usa la misma expresión dos veces → "Try a different one this time." El objetivo es activar las 6 expresiones activamente.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "8 tarjetas, 8 situaciones reales. Eso es un toolkit completo para cualquier Friday update en inglés."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "La tarjeta 7 usa 'achieve' — vocabulario ejecutivo. En S5 usas todo esto en un report de 3 minutos. Ese es tu exit qualification."
§ 03
Listening — The Extended Standup
10 min
🎧
Two-Speaker Monday Standup — Maria + David
ElevenLabs · Maria (Sarah) + David (Adam) · Rate: 0.82 · ~95 sec · Negatives + duration focus

📎 Pega tu URL de Google Drive aquí. Convierte: /view?usp=sharing → /preview

▶ Show ElevenLabs Script

Maria (Sarah, manager) + David (Adam, employee) · Rate 0.82 · ~95 sec


Maria: Good morning David. Quick standup — what happened last week? I see several tasks still open.


David: Good morning. Yes — it was a difficult week. I didn't finish the client proposal. I worked on it from Monday to Thursday, but I didn't receive the final budget numbers from finance. I'll complete it today.


Maria: How long did you spend on it total?


David: Most of the week, honestly. About twelve hours total. But I didn't achieve the quality I wanted without the correct numbers.


Maria: I understand. What about the supplier meeting?


David: I didn't go to the supplier meeting on Wednesday. I had a crisis with the IT system all morning — from nine to noon. I couldn't leave.


Maria: And the client emails?


David: I didn't reply to three client emails. I worked until seven PM on Thursday and didn't have time. I'll respond to all of them before ten today.


Maria: Thank you for being direct, David. Let's make this week different. What's your priority today?


David: The proposal. I'll finish it for three hours this morning and send it before noon.

ElevenLabs: Maria = Sarah (femenina, manager), David = Adam (masculino). Rate 0.82, stability 0.65. Graba separado, combina. Sube a Google Drive con iframe /preview.
"Count every 'didn't' in the audio. How many total?" (Target: 7 — didn't finish, didn't receive, didn't achieve, didn't go, didn't leave/couldn't, didn't reply, didn't have)
Primera escucha: cuenta solo.
"Write down every duration expression you hear." (from Monday to Thursday / most of the week / all morning / from nine to noon / until seven PM)
Segunda escucha: enfoca solo en expresiones de duración.
"How did David explain each thing he didn't do? Was his explanation professional?" Use the model: 'I didn't X because Y. I worked on it for Z.'
Evaluación oral — el alumno reconstruye las 3 explicaciones de David.
"Now YOU are David. Maria asks: 'What happened last week?' — Give YOUR real version. Use 3 negatives + 3 different duration expressions. No notes."
Mínimo 4 oraciones negativas. Al menos 1 irregular (didn't go / didn't have / didn't make).
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Tarea 4 es el diagnóstico principal antes del role play. ¿Puede el alumno producir 3 negativas + 3 expresiones de duración espontáneamente sobre su trabajo real? Si sí → listo para RP completo. Si no → 3 min de drill adicional.
  • David usa "didn't go" (irregular negativo) — señala esto al alumno: "¿Notas que David no dice 'didn't went'? BASE siempre."
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "Maria dice 'Thank you for being direct.' En inglés profesional, la honestidad con detalles es más valorada que evitar el problema. David lo hace bien — ahora tú."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "David dice al final: 'I'll finish it for three hours.' Nota cómo da una cantidad específica de tiempo. Eso es lo que distingue un update vago de uno profesional."
§ 04
Game — Duration Match + Situation Challenge
10–12 min
🎮 Wordwall — External Game
Duration + Negative Sentence Match
🔗 Inserta aquí el link o iframe del Wordwall.
Sugerencia: "Match-Up" — Negative situation ↔ Correct duration expression
Pares: "3-hour meeting" ↔ "for three hours" / "9 AM to noon" ↔ "from 9 to noon" / "whole afternoon" ↔ "all afternoon" / "finished at 8 PM" ↔ "until 8 PM" / "most of the time" ↔ "most of the day" / "from Monday to Wednesday" ↔ "from Monday to Wednesday"
⚠ Include 2 distractor pairs: "during two hours" and "until to 7 PM" — both WRONG. Students must avoid them.
Instrucción: "Match the situation with the correct duration. Watch out for the wrong ones!"
🎯 GT Oral Game — Friday Update Challenge
El maestro da el contexto. El alumno produce la oración completa correcta en menos de 8 segundos.
Round 1 — Duration selection
Situation
"The meeting started at 2 PM and ended at 5 PM." — ¿Cuál expresión usas?
Round 2 — Negative + duration sentence
Situation
"You were in meetings the whole morning. You couldn't reply to the client." — Form the complete professional sentence.
Round 3 — Irregular negative + duration
Situation
"You needed to go to the supplier meeting but stayed at the office working until 7 PM." — Express this correctly.
Round 4 — Tu semana real
El alumno da 3 oraciones reales de su semana — cada una con: negativo + expresión de duración diferente + razón breve. El maestro pregunta una Wh- question después de cada una: "Why didn't you? / How long did it take? / What did you do instead?"
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Round 2: la opción 1 tiene "didn't replied" + "all the morning" — dos errores simultáneos. Si el alumno la elige: enseña ambos errores en secuencia, no al mismo tiempo.
  • Round 3: la opción 1 tiene "didn't went" + "until to" — los dos errores más comunes de S3+S4 juntos. Refuerza: BASE después de didn't, "until" sin "to".
  • Round 4: las Wh- questions del maestro activan S3 + S4 simultáneamente — "Why didn't you?" conecta negativo+razón; "How long did it take?" activa duración.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "Round 4 con datos reales: eso es exactamente tu standup del lunes en inglés. Sin apuntes."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "S5 es el Exit Task — usas todo M1 en un report de 3 min. Negativos + duración son la mitad del contenido. Hoy los tienes automatizados."
§ 05
Role Play — Standup Simulations
12–15 min
Key Phrases — Click to Practise
Honest negative + duration
"I didn't finish — I worked on it for 3 hours, need 2 more."
Uso: reportar tarea incompleta con datos concretos
Meeting negative + duration
"We didn't reach a decision — from 2 to 5 PM, no resolution."
Uso: reportar reunión sin resultado
Communication negative + next steps
"I didn't reply — in meetings all afternoon — I'll respond today."
Uso: explicar emails perdidos profesionalmente
Executive-level negative
"I didn't achieve my target — worked until 7 PM, 3 of 5 done."
Uso: reporte ejecutivo con datos concretos
🎭 Role Play 1 — Friday Update (You + Manager)
Es el viernes a las 5 PM. Tu manager (el maestro) te llama para el update semanal. Debes reportar honestamente lo que NO completaste — con duración y razón — más lo que SÍ lograste. Formato: igual al email de Valeria Torres de S4A.
Tú (Student — Employee)
Reporta: mínimo 3 cosas que no hiciste + duración de cada una. Al menos 1 negativo irregular (didn't go / didn't have / didn't make). Cierra con próximos pasos: "This week I will…"
Manager (Teacher)
Pregunta 5 Wh- questions después de cada negativo: "Why didn't you? / How long? / What did you do instead? / When will you finish? / Who did you mention this to?"
[Manager]: "It's Friday. Before we close the week — what happened? I see several tasks still pending."
Objetivo: el alumno produce 3+ oraciones negativas con duración correcta (for/from-to/all/until), responde Wh- questions sobre sus negativas, y da próximos pasos concretos. Evalúa: ¿"didn't + BASE" consistente? ¿"for" en vez de "during"? ¿"until" sin "to"?
🎭 Role Play 2 — Monday Standup (Recycle listening)
Tú eres David del audio de §3. Tu manager Maria te hace las mismas preguntas — pero ahora respondes con TUS datos reales (no los datos del audio). Un detalle inventado adicional.
David (Student)
Usa la estructura de David: tarea no completada + razón real + duración + próximo paso. Mezcla regulares e irregulares. Mínimo 5 oraciones en pasado.
Maria (Teacher)
"How long did you spend on it?" / "Why didn't you go?" / "When did you receive the last email?" / "What did you achieve last week?"
[Maria]: "Good morning David. Quick standup — what happened last week? I see several tasks still open."
Objetivo: el alumno recicla la estructura del audio con sus propios datos. Nota especial: ¿dice "didn't go" (correcto) o "didn't went" (error de S3)? Corrección inmediata si aparece.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • RP1 usa datos REALES. Si el alumno no sabe cómo decir su tarea en inglés → "Dímela en español, la construimos juntos y luego la repites tú solo."
  • RP2 recicla el audio de §3 con datos propios — el alumno ya conoce la estructura. Evalúa especialmente la espontaneidad de los negativos irregulares.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "Acabas de dar tu Friday update completo en inglés — incluyendo las partes difíciles. Eso es más valioso que solo reportar éxitos."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • Upsell: "S5 = Exit Task. Juntas todo M1 en un report de 3 minutos: regulares, irregulares, negativos, Wh-, duración. Ese es tu exit qualification para el nivel A2.1."
§ 06
Debate — ¿Vale la pena ser honesto en el trabajo?
8–10 min
🗣 Free Speaking — Debate with Negatives + Duration
"Valeria and David were completely transparent — they said exactly what they didn't do and why. Is that always the right strategy in professional life? Or is it sometimes better to say less?"
✅ Always be transparent
"I didn't finish it because… — and I worked on it for 4 hours."
"When I didn't reach the target, I explained the reason."
"My manager didn't fire me — they respected my honesty."
"I didn't achieve it, but I gave 100% effort."
⚠ Sometimes, say less
"I didn't mention the delay — it wasn't important yet."
"We didn't reach a decision, but I didn't explain why."
"Some managers don't want to hear 'I didn't finish.'"
"I worked on it all week — I didn't say it was incomplete."
I didn't finish because… I worked on it for… In my company, we didn't… Last week I didn't achieve… The manager didn't say anything about…
🎤 Optional Extension — Difficult Week Monologue · 2 minutes
"Describe your worst work week this year. What didn't you finish? How long did you work on it? What did you achieve? No notes — 2 minutes."
Mínimo 6 oraciones. Al menos 5 negativas diferentes. Al menos 4 expresiones de duración distintas. Al menos 1 irregular negativo. Cierra con próximos pasos.
Teacher Follow-Up — After Debate
"When did YOU last give a 'difficult' update to your manager? What didn't you achieve? How long had you worked on it?"
"Did your company resolve the issue in the end? How long did it take from start to finish?"
"Think of a task you didn't finish last month. Give me the full professional explanation: didn't + duration + reason + next step."
"What's the longest you ever worked on something you still didn't finish? Use 'from…to' or 'for X hours'."
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • El debate activa negativos en discurso argumentativo auténtico. Regla: el alumno DEBE usar al menos 4 oraciones negativas + 3 expresiones de duración durante el debate. Si no aparecen naturalmente: "Di eso mismo con un ejemplo en pasado."
  • El maestro toma el lado opuesto. Las follow-up questions activan simultáneamente S3 (Wh-) + S4 (negativo + duración).
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "Acabas de debatir cultura laboral en inglés usando didn't, for, until, all afternoon. Eso es inglés ejecutivo A2."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • Testimonial: "Si tu director te preguntara en inglés 'What didn't you finish this week and why?' — ¿podrías responder con datos concretos de duración? Sí. Eso es lo que aprendiste en S4."
