📌 Teacher Follow-Up Questions
"What specific evidence do you have for that strength? Give me a number or a concrete result — 'I've delivered X projects / I've trained X people.'"
"How long have you been working on that weakness? What have you done specifically?"
"If an interviewer asked you about that right now — could you answer in 3 PP sentences without stopping?"
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • S3B — el alumno llega con el vocabulario y la gramática de S3A. Esta sesión es 100% productiva. Si titubea: "You know this formula. Label + 'I've' + evidence. Any PP sentence is a win."
  • Output mínimo aceptable: "I've delivered / I've improved / I've overcome" con al menos un detalle específico = warm-up exitoso al 100%.
  • Error de transición S3A→S3B: el alumno vuelve a usar el label solo sin PP. Recast inmediato: "Great — now add the evidence. What have you delivered specifically?"
  • La imagen que elige el alumno se convierte en el núcleo de su respuesta en §5. Nota qué eligió.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "Last time you learned the formula. Today you use it without the formula in front of you. Cualquier oración PP con evidencia real = win."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "In 50 minutes, you'll answer 6 real interview questions about your strengths and weaknesses — in English, without notes. That's where we're going."

GT Originals — Interview Simulator Scoring
Marca ✓ PP Correct solo cuando el alumno produce una oración PP completa y gramaticalmente correcta con contenido REAL. "I've always been good at delivering" = ✓. "I deliver well" = no PP — presiona de nuevo. Las respuestas modelo aparecen después de cada pregunta — muéstralas solo DESPUÉS de que el alumno hable. Compara: "¿Qué es diferente? ¿Qué PP usó el modelo que tú no usaste?" Q4 (delegating) y Q5 (overcome) son las más difíciles — requieren contenido honesto. Si el alumno desvía: "Be specific. Give me a real situation, not a generic answer."
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • 6 preguntas, ritmo real de entrevista. Avanza después de ~60 segundos por pregunta. No corrijas durante la respuesta — solo marca ✓ o ✗ al final.
  • Q4 y Q5 son pedagógicamente las más valiosas — tocan honestidad real sobre debilidades. Si el alumno se desvía a "I don't have weaknesses": "That's a red flag in interviews. Give me a real one — and what you've done about it."
  • La puntuación de los puntos da un mapa visual de la entrevista. Al terminar: "You scored X/6. Which question was hardest? Let's do that one again — improved version."
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "6 preguntas — entrevista real. Soy el entrevistador. Responde en inglés, oraciones completas. Si yo hubiera pasado al siguiente candidato en una entrevista real, te lo digo."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Estas 6 preguntas cubren la sección completa de strengths and weaknesses de cualquier entrevista de trabajo en inglés. Después de hoy, nunca más tienes que improvisar esta respuesta."
§ 04
Speaking Preparation — Your Full Interview Answer
12 min
Your Mission for §5
Deliver your complete strengths and weaknesses answer to your CEO acting as interviewer. 90 seconds. No notes.
Use Present Perfect at least 8 times.
Include: 2 strengths (label + PP evidence + impact), 1 genuine weakness (honest label + what you've done + progress), and no clichés.
Use at least one "I've been working on" and at least one "I've overcome" or "I've improved."
Interview Scaffold — Organiza tus ideas en los próximos 5 minutos
Strength 1 (opener)
I'd say my biggest strength is [X]. I've [specific PP evidence] — [impact on team / results].
Strength 2
A second strength is [Y]. I've [PP evidence]. I've [result or recognition].
Weakness (honest)
One area I've had to work on is [genuine weakness]. I've been working on this — specifically, I've [concrete action].
Progress Evidence
I've improved: [specific evidence of progress]. I've [overcome / accepted / developed] since then.
Close
Overall, I've built a strong track record in [area] and I've committed to continuous improvement. I'm not perfect — but I've always pushed myself to grow.
8 Advanced Interview Phrases — Haz clic para escuchar
"I'd say my biggest strength is…"
"I'd say my biggest strength is reliability — I've never missed a deadline."
"I've demonstrated this by…"
"I've demonstrated this by leading three high-priority projects simultaneously."
"One area I've had to work on is…"
"One area I've had to work on is delegating — I've always had a tendency to…"
"I've been actively working on…"
"I've been actively working on this for the past year — specifically I've…"
"I've improved significantly in…"
"I've improved significantly in public speaking — I've now led 20+ presentations."
"I've overcome my tendency to…"
"I've overcome my tendency to hold on to tasks — the results have surprised me."
"I've accepted that…"
"I've accepted that asking for feedback is a strength, not a weakness."
"My team has come to depend on…"
"My team has come to depend on my consistency — it's something I've built deliberately."
⏱
5 minutos para prepararte mentalmente. Lee el scaffold. Elige tus 2 fortalezas con evidencia específica. Elige tu 1 debilidad genuina con acción concreta. Identifica qué frases avanzadas usarás. Después cierra todo — en §5, hablas sin notas ni scaffold.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Camina por el scaffold oralmente: "Tell me Strength 1 — label plus evidence." → "Good. Now the weakness — be honest. What have you REALLY had to work on?" → "Now the progress — what specifically have you done?" Este ensayo oral ES la preparación.
  • Mínimo B1.1 para §5: 8+ PP + zero etiquetas sin evidencia + 60 segundos continuos + 1 "I've been working on" + 1 "I've overcome/improved" = mastery completo.
  • Si el alumno elige "perfectionist" como debilidad: coaching rápido — "That's a red flag. Give me a real one. What has your last manager actually given you as development feedback?"
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "El scaffold es tu última red de seguridad. Después de esto — sin tablas, sin pistas. Solo tú y el inglés que ya tienes."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Esta estructura — strength (label + evidence + impact) → weakness (honest label + action + result) — es exactamente lo que los recruiters de empresas internacionales esperan en inglés. La usarás."
§ 05
Speaking Free — Your Real Interview Answer
15 min
You are in a job interview with a senior leader. 90 seconds. No notes. No scaffold. Give your complete strengths and weaknesses answer using Present Perfect at least 8 times. No clichés — real evidence, real growth. Start with: "I'd say my biggest strength is…"
Interviewer Follow-Up Prompts — Usa selectivamente después de los 90 segundos
"Can you give me a specific example — a number, a project, a result?"
"What's the biggest deadline or commitment you've met under real pressure?"
"How long have you been working on that development area?"
"Have you received feedback that confirmed that as a weakness?"
"What has changed — specifically — since you started working on it?"
"Have you ever NOT delivered on a commitment? What happened?"
"What does your team say is your biggest strength? Does it match what you just told me?"
"Where do you still have room to grow — beyond the weakness you mentioned?"
Self-Evaluation — Después de tu presentación
  • I used Present Perfect at least 8 times correctly
  • I gave specific evidence for both strengths (not just labels)
  • I named a genuine weakness — not a cliché
  • I used "I've been working on" or equivalent for the weakness
  • I showed progress on the weakness with PP evidence
  • I spoke for 60+ seconds without stopping
  • I used at least one advanced phrase from §4
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • NO interrumpas durante los 90 segundos. Toma notas de: (a) PP correctos e incorrectos, (b) si evidencia es específica o genérica, (c) si la debilidad es genuina o un cliché, (d) fluencia.
  • Protocolo de feedback: (1) nombra una cosa excelente con ejemplo específico, (2) da una corrección con oración completa, (3) pide que repitan esa oración. Máximo 3 minutos de feedback.
  • Segunda ronda opcional: alumno presenta de nuevo — 60 segundos, mismo contenido, con correcciones incorporadas. La segunda entrega siempre es notablemente más fuerte.
  • Umbral mastery B1.1: 8+ PP + evidencia específica en ambas fortalezas + debilidad genuina + 60+ segundos = mastery completo de S3.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "You just described your professional strengths and weaknesses in English — in Present Perfect — for 90 seconds. That is a complete interview answer. That is genuinely rare."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "In S4A you'll apply this same self-awareness to your personality type — introvert vs extrovert — using 'I've found that…' and 'I've noticed…'"

Speaking Challenge — Closing
Storytelling · No Scaffold · 60–90 sec

"Tell me about the most meaningful professional development moment of your career so far — a time when you acknowledged a real weakness and turned it into growth. Use Present Perfect to frame it throughout."

No cards, no scaffold. One story. Maximum impact. PP development patterns throughout. Aim for 5+ uses in 60 seconds. Include: "I've had to… I've been working on… I've improved / overcome… What I've learned is…"

GT Originals — Mastery Session Tips
Contrasta el warm-up vs. el closing challenge. Nombra la diferencia específica: "En el warm-up diste el label. Ahora das el label + evidencia + progreso. Esa diferencia es exactamente lo que separa A2 de B1.1." Si el §5 fue fuerte: "That answer would land in any English-speaking interview. You sound like someone who knows their own performance — and that's exactly what international companies want." Spot-check irregular: "Give me 'overcome' in PP right now." Si sale 'overcome' instantáneo (no 'overcame') → participle internalizado.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • FCW Ronda 1: 4 verbos de desarrollo, 90 segundos. El alumno produce la oración PP antes de hacer clic. Evalúa: participle correcto, no specific-time error, evidencia específica.
  • FCW Ronda 2: 4 tarjetas de frases. El alumno completa con contenido REAL de su carrera. Esto hace las frases personales y mucho más probable que se retengan.
  • Closing challenge: 5+ PP en 60 segundos, zero etiquetas sin evidencia, narrativa coherente. Si los tres = mastery completo de S3 B1.1.
Capa 2 · Engagement

  • Testimonial trigger: "Give me your strength answer right now — no preparation, no notes. One sentence." Si sale limpio con PP y evidencia → "THAT is your testimonial. That's what B1.1 sounds like."
