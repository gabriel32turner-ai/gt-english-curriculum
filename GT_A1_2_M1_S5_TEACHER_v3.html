ON / IN / AT Errors
✗ "In Monday" → ✅ ON Monday
✗ "On March" → ✅ IN March

✗ "Every Mondays" → ✅ Every Monday

Activity 2 — True or False: M1 Rules
Is this grammar rule correct?
In English, "twice" means the same as "two times".
"Please to confirm" is the correct polite imperative form.
We use ON with specific dates: "on the 15th" / "on March 20th".
In the question "How often does she send?", the verb "send" adds -s.

Drag the pieces to make a correct sentence using M1 grammar.
      Capa 1 · Pedagogical

      Reading

      Mónica Herrera
      Sales Manager · ConnectPro International
      Find as you read: ordinal numbers · ON/IN/AT · frequency adverbs · frequency expressions · imperatives · weather small talk · 3rd person -s

      My name is Mónica Herrera and I am a Sales Manager at ConnectPro International. My work is very dynamic — I work with clients in the United States and Canada.

      My week usually starts on Monday morning with a team standup at 8:30 AM. I always prepare my agenda the night before. Our sales review is on the 1st and the 15th of every month — I never miss these.

      I have international calls twice a week — usually on Tuesdays and Thursdays. When I call clients in Chicago, I always open with small talk: "How's the weather over there?" It makes the call warmer. It is usually sunny and warm here — but freezing in Chicago in winter!

      For my team, I always give clear instructions: "Send all reports before Friday at 5 PM. Don't use personal email for client documents. Please confirm all appointments by Wednesday."

      My coordinator Ana sends me a progress update every Monday. She also prepares the agenda for our calls. She always meets her targets — her performance is excellent.

      Our performance review is three times a year: in April, in August, and in December. The next one is on the 22nd of April at 10 AM — please put it in your calendar!

      Comprehension — Full M1
      Answer the questions.
      How often does Mónica have international calls?
      When is the next performance review?
      Ana always meets her targets.
      Mónica sometimes misses the sales review meetings.
      Ana sends a progress update every Monday.
      Capa 1 · Pedagogical
      • 3rd person verbs highlighted in green (sends, prepares, meets) — ask student to find them and read those sentences aloud.
      • All 4 M1 grammar points appear: frequency adverbs, ON/IN/AT, 3rd person -s, imperatives. Ask student to find one example of each.
      • R2 trap: "in April at 8:30 AM" — the text says "8:30 AM" for the standup, not the review. The review is at 10 AM on the 22nd.

      GT Originals — Module 1 Highlights
      ⚠️ "twice" = 2× · "once" = 1× · "three times" = 3+. Never "two times" in professional English.
      📞 30 seconds of weather small talk before business = better relationships. Never optional on international calls.
