§1

🔥 Hot Take — Cold Start

5 min
No prep. No notes. Just speak — 60 seconds starts NOW.
Regla de sesión B: El alumno habla SIN preparación. No hay tiempo para pensar. 60 segundos cold start. Esto activa todo lo aprendido en S1A bajo presión real.
Si el alumno se detiene: "Keep going! Use any word you know. What do you see at an airport?"
🔥 Hot Take — 60 seconds
You arrive at Querétaro Airport for a business trip to Chicago. Describe everything you do: check-in, boarding pass, gate, plane. GO!
60 sec max No notes No Spanish No pauses > 5 sec
Después: "What was ONE word you used that you are proud of?" → Refuerza antes de corregir. Luego señala UNA forma para mejorar.
§2

🎯 Pitch — Assigned Sides

10 min
You don't choose your side — you defend what you're given. Real English.
Regla: El alumno NO elige su lado — tú lo asignas. Esto rompe la zona de confort.
Topic: "It is better to receive a client at your office than to visit them at theirs."
🟣 Side A — AGREE
"Receiving clients at YOUR office is better. Defend this for 90 seconds."
Starters: "I think it is better because you can help them and show them…" "At my office, I can give them coffee, Wi-Fi, and a badge…" "I know the meeting room and I can call my team if I need them…"
🔵 Side B — DISAGREE
"Visiting the CLIENT is better. Counter the other side for 90 seconds."
Starters: "I don't agree. When I visit the client, I can see their office…" "If I travel to them, they know I care about the meeting…" "The client doesn't need to travel — it is easier for them…"
§3

🔄 Relay — Transform It

8 min
No support. No grammar table. Just you and the language.
Instrucción: "I give you a sentence. You change it. No notes. Speak out loud." Las transformaciones usan object pronouns — target de S1A — ahora en producción espontánea.
Dinámica: T→S — Teacher lee, alumno transforma.
Transform 1 — Replace with pronoun
"Please call the manager when the visitor arrives."
→ Replace "the manager" with a pronoun. Say the full sentence.
"Please call him when the visitor arrives."
Transform 2 — Replace with pronoun
"Can you send Mónica the Wi-Fi password?"
→ Replace "Mónica" with a pronoun.
"Can you send her the Wi-Fi password?"
Transform 3 — Replace TWO words
"Give the visitors the badges at reception."
→ Replace "the visitors" AND "the badges" with pronouns.
"Give them to them." or better: "Give the badges to them." / "Give them the badges."
Transform 4 — Fix the error
"Call to me when you arrive at the hotel."
→ This sentence has an error. Fix it and say it correctly.
"Call me when you arrive at the hotel." (no "to" after "call")
Transform 5 — Your reality
"Mónica helps the client with his luggage."
→ Say what YOU do when a client arrives at YOUR office.
Open answer — student uses "I help him/her with…" + real context from their workplace.

⚡ Speed Round — Say It Fast

6 min
Click → read → say a FULL sentence. No pauses. No translation.
Reglas: Alumno click → ve la palabra → produce UNA oración completa en 20 seg. 7+ tarjetas = badge ⚡.
Ejemplo: "badge" → "Please give him a visitor badge."

🎤 Solo Challenge — 2 Minutes

8 min
Two minutes. No notes. No help. Just you and English.
Solo Challenge: El alumno habla 2 minutos continuo sobre el escenario. Sin notas, sin ayuda. El teacher solo escucha y toma notas de errores para después. Badge 🎤 al completar los 2 min.
Si se detiene antes de 1 min: Prompt: "What else? Where is the meeting room? What do you offer?"
🎤 SOLO — 2 Minutes Nonstop
Scenario: A new client from Germany arrives at your company for the first time. You are the host. Welcome them from the front door to the meeting room. Include:
Greeting Badge + sign in Directions Offer: water/coffee Wi-Fi info 3+ object pronouns

🔍 Source Analysis — Real Messages

8 min
Read real messages. Understand them. Answer questions.
Dinámica: Alumno lee cada cita → responde la pregunta en voz alta → click en la opción. Las preguntas abiertas no tienen respuesta correcta — solo las cerradas. T→S para análisis.
📧 Email from reception
"Good morning, Mr. Chen. Welcome to Solaris. Please sign in here and I will give you a visitor badge. Can I help you with your bags?"
❓ What does the receptionist offer to do?
💬 WhatsApp from colleague
"The client is at the lobby. Can you come and escort him to the meeting room? I am on a call. Tell him I will be there in 10 minutes."
❓ Who should escort the client to the meeting room?
📞 Hotel voicemail
"Good afternoon, this is the Fiesta Inn Querétaro. We have your reservation for two nights. Please call us to confirm your check-in time. Our number is 442-555-0190."
❓ What does the hotel want?
✈️ Airport announcement
"Attention passengers on flight 2847 to Chicago. There is a 45-minute delay. Please wait at gate B12. We will give you an update soon."
❓ What happened to the flight?
