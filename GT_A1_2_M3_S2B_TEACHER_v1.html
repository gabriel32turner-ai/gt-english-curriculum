§1

🔥 Hot Take — Cold Start

5 min
No prep. No map. Give directions — 60 seconds NOW.
Cold start rule: El alumno habla sin preparación. Si se detiene: "Turn left or right? How far? What landmark?" — prompts de dirección, no de vocabulario.
Error a vigilar: "Go to right" → "Turn right". Corrige al final, no mid-utterance.
🔥 Hot Take — 60 sec
A client just landed at the airport. They call you and say: "I'm at the exit. How do I get to your office?" Give complete directions. NOW — no notes, no map!
60 sec No notes Use direction imperatives Use "How long"
Debrief: "What was ONE good direction word you used?" → reinforce. Then: "What was missing?" → one fix only.
§2

🎯 Pitch — Assigned Sides

10 min
WhatsApp directions vs phone call — which is better for a foreign visitor?
Topic: "It is better to send directions by WhatsApp than to explain them by phone." Teacher asigna lados — el alumno NO elige. Push for "through", "past", "turn", "go straight" in both arguments.
Time: 90 seconds per side, then 60-second open debate.
🟣 Side A — WHATSAPP IS BETTER
"Sending directions by WhatsApp is better. Defend this for 90 seconds."
Starters: "By WhatsApp, the client can read: 'Turn right at the traffic lights…'" "If the directions are written, the client doesn't forget them…" "You can send a map — it's easier than 'go past the Oxxo'…"
🔵 Side B — PHONE IS BETTER
"Explaining by phone is better. Counter the other side."
Starters: "By phone, you can say: 'Now turn left, go through the gate'…" "If the client is lost, you can help them in real time…" "Not all clients use WhatsApp — a phone call works for everyone…"
§3

🔄 Relay — Transform the Direction

8 min
No notes. No grammar table. Just transform and speak.
Dinámica: Teacher da la instrucción → alumno transforma en voz alta. Sin notas. Énfasis en prepositions of movement y direction imperatives bajo presión.
Si el alumno se bloquea: "What's the opposite of 'left'? OK — now say the full sentence."
Transform 1 — Change direction
"Turn left at the traffic lights."
→ Change to the opposite direction.
"Turn right at the traffic lights."
Transform 2 — Fix the preposition
"Go along customs and show your passport."
→ "Along" is wrong here. Fix it and say the correct sentence.
"Go through customs and show your passport." — You enter and exit customs, so "through".
Transform 3 — Change transport
"Take a taxi to the office. It's 40 minutes."
→ Change to Uber. Keep the rest.
"Take an Uber to the office. It's 40 minutes."
Transform 4 — How far → How long
"How far does it take from the airport?"
→ This question has an error. Fix it and answer it.
"How long does it take from the airport?" — "How far" = distance. "How long" = time. Then answer: "About 45 minutes."
Transform 5 — Your office, your directions
"Go past the Oxxo and the office is on the left."
→ Replace "Oxxo" with a real landmark near YOUR office. Say the full sentence.
Open answer — student uses a real landmark: "Go past the [their landmark] and the office is on the [left/right]."

⚡ Speed Round — Landmark to Direction

6 min
Click a landmark → say a FULL direction sentence. No pauses. 7+ = badge!
Reglas: Alumno hace click → teacher dice la landmark en voz alta → alumno produce una oración de dirección completa en 20 seg. 7+ tarjetas completadas = badge ⚡.
Ejemplo: "traffic lights" → "Turn left at the traffic lights." / "customs" → "Go through customs."

§5

🎤 Solo Challenge — 2 Minutes

8 min
Two minutes. No notes. Full role-play — airport to office.
Solo: El alumno hace el role-play completo. Teacher juega el rol del cliente. Hace preguntas reales durante el role-play ("Wait — turn left or right?" / "How long from the roundabout?"). Badge 🎤 al completar 2 minutos con comunicación clara.
Mínimo aceptable: Transporte + 3 instrucciones de dirección + "How long".
🎤 SOLO ROLE-PLAY — Full Reception Protocol
Scenario: A client from the US lands at BJX Airport. They call you on arrival. You give complete directions AND tell them what to do when they arrive at the office.
Transport from airport 3+ direction steps At least 2 prepositions of movement How long / how far What to do at reception

🔍 Source Analysis — Real Direction Messages

8 min
Read real WhatsApp messages. Find the errors. Fix them.
Dinámica: Alumno lee → identifica el error o responde la pregunta → click. Preguntas abiertas → discusión. T→S para análisis de error.
Conversation 1 — Supplier arrives at BJX
Client (Mr. Davis)
Hi! I'm at the airport exit. How do I get there?
Mónica
Take a taxi from Celaya. Then turn right in the traffic lights. Go past to the big Oxxo and you're here! 😊
❓ Mónica's message has 3 wrong prepositions. Can you find them?
Conversation 2 — Hotel check-in question
Client
How far does it take from the hotel to your office?
You
About 15 minutes by taxi. It's not far!
❓ The client's question has an error. What is it?
Conversation 3 — Perfect directions? You decide.
Roberto
From the airport, take the shuttle to Terminal B. Go through the arrivals exit. Walk along the taxi rank and take the first taxi. Tell the driver: ConnectPro, Celaya. It's about 40 minutes. You can't miss it!
❓ How many prepositions of movement does Roberto use correctly?
Conversation 4 — Decode the directions
Client
I'm at the roundabout near the highway. What now?
Mónica
Take the second exit. Go straight for 2 km. Go past the HSBC bank. We are the blue building on the right. 🏢
❓ Which direction command does Mónica NOT use in this message?
